## The Red Wall Security
Our security model, internally known as "The Red Wall," uses multi-layered isolation to protect your data.

- **Zero-Trust Neural Architecture**: Every request is verified, even within the internal fabric.
- **Dynamic Encryption**: Keys are cycled periodically based on neural patterns, making traditional brute-force attacks impossible.
- **Hardware Isolation**: Critical computing cores are isolated from the public-facing gateway via the "Black Layer."

## Anomaly Detection
Using Gemini AI, we monitor every "pulse" in the fabric. If a data pattern deviates from the norm, APEX immediately isolates the affected node and alerts the system administrator.

## Compliance
Despite our retro look, we maintain modern security standards, ensuring your data remains private and protected according to global regulations.
